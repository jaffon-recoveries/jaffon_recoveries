Security Questions and Verification:


Some services may ask you to answer security questions or provide additional information to verify your identity. This may include providing a backup email address, phone number, or security codes sent to your mobile device. Security questions are a common method used for identity verification during account recovery. These are questions that you answered when you initially set up your account. Common security questions might include: Providing the last known password for the account. Confirming details related to your account, such as the date you created it. Identifying recent account activity, such as the last time you accessed the account. Supplying information related to the account's security settings, like backup email addresses or phone numbers
